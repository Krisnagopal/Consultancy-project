
<h1> Google BI Project </h1>

<h2>Description</h2>
This consultancy project has been completed as a requirement in the Business Research & Consultancy course in my MSc program. Here, I worked as a junior consultant in a team of four(04) students for an external company under the supervision of course coordinators. We worked as consultants for the Groningen City Club (GCC) to help them finding optimum solutions for a problem situation. The problem was centered around the Groningen City Retailers  
<br />


<h2>Languages Used</h2>

- <b>SQL for ETL operation </b> 

<h2>Tools and Libraries Used</h2>

- <b>Google BigQuery for extracting, transforming and loading data into a final destination </b>
- <b>Tableau for creating visualizations and Dashboards using the final data </b>
